## HydraFacial: The Ultimate Skin Resurfacing Treatment

### How HydraFacial Works

Our advanced HydraFacial device uses a unique vortex-fusion delivery system to gently resurface your skin. The treatment removes dead skin cells and impurities while simultaneously infusing the skin with intensive moisturizers, peptides, and antioxidants. The multi-step process includes:

**Cleanse + Peel** - Uncovers a new layer of skin with gentle exfoliation and resurfacing
**Extract + Hydrate** - Removes debris from pores using painless suction while nourishing with intense moisturizers
**Fuse + Protect** - Saturates the skin's surface with antioxidants and peptides to maximize your glow
